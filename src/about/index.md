## Dr. Theodore Davis, M.S., D.C.

Dr. Davis received his Doctorate in Chiropractic Medicine from Logan College of Chiropractic in Chesterfield, MO. He grew up in Washington State. As well as receiving his Masters Degree in Sports Science and Rehabilitation from Logan College of Chiropractic in 2011. He did his internship with the University of Denver Sports Medicine Department.

He also received his certification in Acupuncture from Texas Chiropractic College and received his Bachelor of Science in Biology from the University of Washington in Seattle.

Dr. Davis has worked with many collegiate, professional, and Olympic level athletes, and has also managed hundreds of automotive injury cases with many of the most successful law firms in Colorado.

He is also trained to work with infants, young children, pregnant women, and geriatric patients. Dr. Davis does not believe that you need to hear a “crack” or “pop” to achieve an adjustment. With delicate individuals, delicate forms of treatment are often required.

Dr. Davis is highly trained in various forms of advanced soft tissue and spine-related treatments and rehabilitation modalities. He specializes in the management and treatment of disc lesions, overuse soft tissue injuries, traumatic injuries, pain management, and peripheral neuropathies. He is licensed to perform acupuncture, chiropractic, as well as physiotherapy, and electro-physiotherapy in the state of Colorado.
